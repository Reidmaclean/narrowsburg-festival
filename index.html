The Dream

Colin and Reid met in 1977. First day of first grade. They happened to be wearing the same Hush Puppies, which gave Colin the perfect ice breaker. That was the beginning of a lifelong friendship that at its heart has had music, friends, epic parties and always a running conversation about the future. What we'd do.

And at the core of that was a dream to bring people together with music. We used to call it The Farm. An artist retreat center. A rolling performance. A Hootenanny. The dream has shape shifted over time. But the last few years the idea of a festival has taken root.

Who doesn't wish to go to the festival. Sondheim got it. We bet you will too.

You should know that most of you receiving this invite are the lineup. A music festival for musicians. Let us know your current artistic incarnation and what kind of set you'd like to play. We'll figure out if it's a woodsy vibe, a barn burner, or a sing-along by the river. Lots of possibilities — we're dreamscaping it now.

Is it me, or have we not seen each other for a long time? Let's change that. Come play.
